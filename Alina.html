ARE YOU READY?

Do you love your Hyper friends? Do you love skiing? Do you hate the feeling of FOMO? If you answer yes on any of those three, you have to join the HI ski trip!!!!!

WHO? All current Hyper students
WHEN? 15-18 March 2018
WHERE? Idre (6 hours north of Stockholm)
TRANSPORT? Busses will leave from Stockholm city and go straight to Idre.
LODGING? We will rent houses in Idre, with room for 8-18 people in each apartment.

The price will differ depending on how many that wants to participate, but the price for bus + lodging will be approx. 2000 SEK.
Ski pass (3 days) = 870 SEK
Ski rental = fr. 390 SEK

For those who don't like to ski there are lots of other things to do in Idre:
- go tobogganing
- dog sled riding
- play boardgames and enjoy a "glögg-fylla" (get drunk on mulled wine)
- hot tub/sauna-bathing (like a proper northerner)
- cuddle up with books, wine and Hyper friends in the cottages
- snow ball fighting
More info on how you'll sign up and pay will be out soon! LIMITED SPOTS AVAILABLE!
